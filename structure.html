Format of The Tournament
  • 36 of the very best clubs qualify for the UCL
  • In the first round every club is put in a league phase and plays 8 games
  • The Top 8 Clubs automatically qualify for the Round of 16
  • Those from 9-16th position are seeded against those who are 17-24th in the RO16 Playoffs
  • After this the top 8 are seeded against the playoff winners in the RO16
  • From here the knockout stage prgresses from RO16 to QFs to SFs and then the Final
